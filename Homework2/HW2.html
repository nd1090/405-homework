My peanut butter and fluff with banana sandwhich recipe

Details here

Directions

  1. First grab ingrediants
    • Grab two pieces of bread
    • slice banana
    • Grab peanut butter
    • Grab fluff
  2. Make the food
    • put 2 tablespoons of fluff on one side of the bread
    • put 2 tablespoons of peanut butter on the other side of the bread
    • slice banana and apply on both sides of bread
    • put both pieces of bread together
    • Eat the Sandwhich
